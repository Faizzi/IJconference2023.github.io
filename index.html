Developing the Future of Justice Technologies

The conference brings together an elite group of jurists and interested people around the world to exchange knowledge, legal expertise, and legal enrichment to strengthen the foundations of justice around the world.

Conference Message

“The jurists discuss the future of Justice technologies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia”

Conference Goals

The Conference is going to provide a wonderful platform to discuss the challenges confronting the justice sector and offer deeper insights to determine the way forward and the future policy course in the delivery of expeditious justice for citizens of Saudi Arabia

The future of judicial technologies and digital transformation in the legal field.

Prospects for the investment of artificial intelligence technologies in the development of judicial work.

The future of international cooperation in the field of justice.

International experiences in judicial cooperation and draft model conventions on judicial cooperation between countries.

Why are we discussing the future of justice technologies?

The world is witnessing significant developments in the field of justice, particularly in relation to supporting and enhancing judicial work, and in line with the latest international experiences and digital transformations in this regard, the Saudi Ministry of Justice organizes the International Justice Conference, which brings together a selection of lawyers from more than 30 countries around the world over two days to exchange knowledge and experiences and to discuss opinions and enrichment, which enhances justice and rights in the world and expands relations between countries in the field of justice.
